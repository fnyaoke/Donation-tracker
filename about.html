About Us



For organizations and individuals looking to positively impact their communities, finding the exact community is quite the challenge Tracking the movement and getting verification of the donation is also a task that some start-up initiatives are not able to do. This widens the gap between those that can receive support and those that may not receive support dueto either low visibility or lack of skills to meet the donors demands. Donation tracker is just the site to bridge this gap.

Donation tracker is a site that links the donors to the communities that need support, while offering a platform to increase the visibility of the communities/initiatives needing support

The site will show you the places in need, mapping out their regions and showing the kind of support they have received.

This allows donors to not only reach wider communities, but also gives a recording of those that need support urgently. The site also prints out a report for both the donor and the initiative/community!
